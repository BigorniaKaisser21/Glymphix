1. Who We Are (Data Controller)

Glymphix ("we", "our", "us") is a web application developed by Studyholics that converts images of handwritten or printed code into editable text using optical character recognition (OCR) technology.

As the personal information controller (PIC) under RA 10173, we are responsible for ensuring your personal data is collected, used, and protected lawfully.

Contact: privacy@glymphix.app

Address: Philippines

Images are not permanently stored. Uploaded image files are deleted from our servers immediately after your analysis is saved. Only the extracted text result is retained.

3. How and Why We Use Your Data

  • Providing the service — creating and managing your account, performing OCR analysis, saving and displaying your results.
  • Authentication and security — verifying your identity via OTP email, preventing unauthorised access, detecting abuse.
  • Communications — sending OTP verification codes and password-reset codes to your email address.
  • Service improvement — understanding aggregate usage patterns to improve OCR accuracy (no individual profiling).
  • Legal compliance — retaining records as required by applicable Philippine law.

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal data to any third party for marketing purposes.

4. Legal Basis for Processing

Under Section 12 of RA 10173, we process your personal data on the following bases:

  • Contract performance — processing necessary to deliver the Glymphix service you signed up for.
  • Consent — for optional data (e.g. Google Sign-In profile picture) and cookies. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legitimate interest — security monitoring and fraud prevention, where your interests are not overridden.
  • Legal obligation — where processing is required by Philippine law.

5. Who We Share Your Data With

  • Google LLC — if you use Google Sign-In, your authentication is handled by Google under their own privacy policy.
  • Email provider (Gmail SMTP) — used solely to deliver OTP and password-reset emails.
  • Hosting infrastructure — our servers store your data. We do not share identifiable data with cloud analytics services.

We do not transfer personal data outside the Philippines except where necessary for the above services (Google, Gmail), which are covered by appropriate safeguards.

7. How We Protect Your Data

  • Password hashing — passwords are stored using PBKDF2-SHA256 (via Werkzeug). We never store passwords in plaintext.
  • Encrypted transport — all data is transmitted over HTTPS with HSTS enforced.
  • Session security — session cookies are HttpOnly (not readable by JavaScript), SameSite=Lax (CSRF mitigation), and Secure (HTTPS only in production).
  • Rate limiting — login, OTP, and password-reset endpoints are rate-limited to prevent brute-force attacks.
  • OTP lockout — accounts are locked after 5 incorrect OTP attempts.
  • Access controls — users can only access their own analyses; server-side ownership checks are applied on every request.
  • Minimal retention — uploaded images are deleted immediately after processing.

8. Your Rights Under RA 10173

As a data subject, you have the following rights under the Philippine Data Privacy Act (Section 16):

Right to be informed

Know what data we collect, why, and how it is used — this Privacy Policy fulfils that right.

Right of access

Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you by contacting our DPO.

Right to correction

Update your name or username in your profile settings at any time.

Right to erasure

Delete your account and all associated data permanently via your Profile page.

Right to object

Object to processing of your data for purposes beyond the core service by contacting us.

Right to data portability

Request an export of your analysis history. Contact our DPO to request this.

Right to withdraw consent

Withdraw consent for optional processing at any time without affecting past processing.

Right to lodge a complaint

File a complaint with the National Privacy Commission (NPC) at privacy.gov.ph.

To exercise any of these rights, email us at privacy@glymphix.app with the subject line "Data Subject Request". We will respond within 15 business days.

9. Cookies and Session Data

We use a single essential session cookie (session) to keep you logged in. This cookie:

  • Is strictly necessary for the service to function — you cannot use Glymphix without it.
  • Is not used for tracking or advertising.
  • Is deleted when you log out or close your browser (it is not a persistent tracking cookie).

We do not use third-party advertising cookies, analytics trackers, or fingerprinting scripts.

10. Children's Privacy

Glymphix is intended for students and educators. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13 years of age without verifiable parental consent. If you believe a child has registered without consent, please contact us immediately so we can delete the account.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. When we make significant changes, we will:

  • Update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page.
  • Display an in-app notice to logged-in users on their next visit.

Continued use of Glymphix after the effective date constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
