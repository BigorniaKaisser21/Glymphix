Glymphix is an educational tool that uses optical character recognition (OCR) technology to convert images of handwritten or printed code into editable text. The Service provides:

Educational purpose. Glymphix is designed to assist students and educators. OCR results may not be 100% accurate and should always be reviewed before use in academic submissions.
